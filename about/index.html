Introduction

If you are here and not lost, let me introduce myself. I am Lalith Nayan, an Information Security professional living in the San Francisco Bay Area. I love to learn new things and unlearn old ideas and habits that are no longer relevant. I will be using this blog to reduce mind clutter and maybe as a side effect , teach you few things along the way in this journey called ’life’!

My main goal of this blog can be summarized as ‘Controlled Chaos’. Each day we consume a lot of information (Chaos) that does not translate into anything meaningful. I believe that by dedicating some focussed time (especially early mornings :D) and being result oriented (the ‘Why’ question), one can gain meaningful insights (Controlled) that can help stay organized and lead a better life!!

What will I write about?

In the current trend of rehashing the same information for clicks and inflated phrasing that misses the damn point, I am hoping to be ‘original’ (whatever that means). My simple approach will be to stick to the domains I will be doing research in. I love reading books and research papers and synthesizing information into a consumable format, discovering new music and following leaders and understanding their thought process. I will definitely be writing about my one true love ‘Information Security’.
